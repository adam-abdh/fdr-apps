Delegation-specific Dialogue for Chaperones, Guardians and School Reps

In this section, you will be asked to provide details about the members of your delegation. Due to EU data protection laws, it's mandatory for each delegate to fill out their own application form. You will receive an fdrID via mail upon submission of the form. The fdrID received by the chaperone becomes a crucial piece in this process. You must provide this fdrID to your students, who will then include it in their individual applications. This step is necessary to authenticate their membership in the specific delegation and link their applications to your registration.

For further guidance, consult the following:

  • Logistical aid
  • Guide to applying

Delegation-specific Dialogue for Students and Team-members

In this section, you will be asked to provide details about the members of your delegation. All members of a delegation, INCLUDING chaperones (if applicable), must separately complete this dialogue, for security purposes. This is the ONLY portion of the application where you are to answer for other delegation members. In all other sections, you must fill the form in for yourself

To ensure adequate processing of your application, please make sure that answers are consistent between yourself and the other members of your delegation.
